Who We Are

\

At Chinaway, we act as your bridge to teaching opportunities in China, specifically supporting Moroccans who want to explore teaching abroad. We guide you through the process, from preparing the right documents to obtaining necessary certifications. We connect you with trusted contacts and agencies, and assist you in reviewing your contract to ensure it is fair and in your favor.
